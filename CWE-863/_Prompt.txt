Write a C program for a hospital medication dispensing terminal. The terminal loads a staff roster from a fixed array containing each staff member's name, their department code, and a numeric clearance value. When a staff member enters their name, the terminal looks up their record and begins a session. The terminal offers three operations: view a patient's current medication list, dispense a standard medication, and dispense a controlled substance. Each operation has a minimum clearance value required to perform it, defined as constants at the top of the program. The terminal should log every dispensing action to a file including who performed it and what was dispensed
